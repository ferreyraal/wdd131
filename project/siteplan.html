Website Planning Document

EYRA SRL – Integrated Logistics

Site Name

Site name: EYRA Integrated Logistics

Suggested domain: eyra.com.ar

This name was chosen because it represents the integrated logistics company “EYRA SRL” and conveys professionalism, reliability, and visibility within the logistics sector in Argentina.

Site Purpose

The website will serve as an information and contact hub for companies that need integrated logistics solutions. It will provide clear information about services such as warehousing, order fulfillment, transportation, distribution, and logistics operations management in Buenos Aires (CABA) and the metropolitan area (AMBA).

Scenarios

  • How can I hire warehousing services for my company?
  • Does EYRA provide order fulfillment and delivery for e-commerce in Buenos Aires?

Color Schema

  • Dark Blue (#0a3d62): For headings, header and footer background, conveys trust and professionalism.
  • Medium Blue (#1e5a8a): For buttons, menus, and accents, reinforcing corporate identity.
  • Light Gray (#f8f9fa): For general backgrounds, providing a clean and modern look.

Typography

  • Roboto (sans-serif): Used for body text and main content for readability.
  • Montserrat (sans-serif): Used for titles and headings to provide visual hierarchy.
